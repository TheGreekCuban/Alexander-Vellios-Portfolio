About Me

Full Stack Developer leveraging a background in real estate and hospitality management to build streamlined and intuitive applications that will impact a business' bottom line. Alexander is a creative thinker that consistently seeks optimization and has an insatiable hunger for organization.

Alexander discovered his passion for coding in June, 2018. He has since earned a certificate in Full Stack Web Development from Columbia University in New York City.

Alexander has built various applications both alone and with partners. Alexander was consistently a main contributor in all team projects, which were built on the MERN stack. Given the opportunity, he likes to focus on back-end development. This preference is illustrated in his contributions to the group projects. He looks forward to applying this newly gained skill set in the future on a quality driven team that consistently raises the bar for their colleagues while simultaneously making the workplace a more enjoyable place for everyone he interacts with!

Front End Skills:
HTML5 | CSS3 | Javascript | JQuery | Bootstrap | Materialize | Handlebars.js | React.js

Back End Skills:
Node.js | MySQL | MongoDB | Express.js | Sequelize.js | Model View Controller Format | ORM | RESTful API

In addtion, Alexander also attended The University of Delaware's Alfred Lerner College of Business and Economics where he graduated with a Bachelors of Science in Hotel, Restaurant and Institutional Management.
